Title                   : Conway's Game of Life
Filename                : conway.zip
Release date            : November 6th, 2022
Author                  : Piotr Wieczorek
Email Address           : pwiecz@gmail.com

Description             : Implementation of Conway's Game of Life in Boom
                          compatible Doom engine.

			  Step over the raised panels to make respective 
			  cells alive, then pull the switch to start the
			  simulation.

Additional Credits to   : Wouter van Oortmerssen and Jonathan Downland -
	                  creators of WadC
